About

Derived from the 3D soft­ware Blender, Blenderei (“blendery”) stands for free artistic and also applied projects in the field of 3D graphics and animation. Blenderei is operated by me, Silke Fischer-Imsieke.

I've studied art at the Academy of Fine Arts in Leipzig. After graduating I've specialized in 3D graphics and animation. I like to experiment with the possibilities Blender provides and also love the inspiring process of working together with other professionals. I’m working in the sphere of character design, modeling, texturing, 2D and 3D animation, and exhibition design. Furthermore I'm especially interested in the conceptual integration of 3D elements into contemporary art.
